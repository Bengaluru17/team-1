About

Kshamata ‘s mission is to touch lives and bring liberation by empowering women and adolescent girls faced with vulnerable and exploitative situations to become respected,productive and earning citizens by providing opportunities for training, employment and reintegration in society.

Kshamata’s vision is a world free of exploitation, where women can live confident lives, with a clear sense of positive identity and self-worth
